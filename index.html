Projects

My recent projects include a water ordering system, a barangay information system, and an attendance checker, each designed with user-friendly interfaces and practical functionality. Here are some UI/UX highlights from my work.

First Project

Water Ordering System

This system streamlines the process of ordering and delivering water refills for households and businesses.

Second Project

Barangay Information System

The Barangay Information System is designed to efficiently manage resident records, maintain barangay profiles, and streamline the delivery of services.

Third Project

Attendance Checker App

This app simplifies student attendance tracking using QR codes, enabling class mayors to manage attendance efficiently and generate reports. The system is built with Android Studio and Firebase.
